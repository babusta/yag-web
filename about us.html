Who we are

Ye.Are.gods initiative believes that the knowlege of ones identity is the headway to creating the change we need in the world. we try our best to reach out to all youth in each community in Nigeria. we nuture them and train them till they start their own projects.

What we do

we execute programs that help the Nigeria youth in various ways. by doing this, we take them off the street,provide facilities and resources and guide them to provide creative solutions to change a current situation in the environment. we believe we are building nation builders.

Our Mission

To empower youths and help them discover who they in christ in other to fulfill purpose.
